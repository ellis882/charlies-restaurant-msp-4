About Charlie's Grillhouse

The dream of Charlie's Grillhouse was born on a amazing family trip in Brazil. We always had loved the Brazilian way of cooking. Three years later and a lot of tasting, we opened our doors in New York.

  • At Charlie's you can eat the best steaks in town
  • Every saturday we offer you an all-you-can-eat rodizio in an authentic Brazilian way
  • We love to party with our house-dj every last friday of the month

So let's book a table, plan an event with us or just come to enjoy our restaurant and have a great evening. See you soon at Charlie's!

Why choose our Restaurant

Charlie's Grillhouse is a friendly, informal and typical Brazilian grillhouse restaurant to come with your friends or with your family to have a great evening. Every saturday we have a rodizio (all-you-can-eat buffet) and all the other days you can choose from the menu.

01

Our Famous Rodizio

Only on saturday you can come for our rodizio where you start with a buffet. You pay a fixed price and our gauchos will bring you the best peace of meat by choice.

02

Friendly Environment

Our motto is to make the atmosphere friendly and as efficient as possible. The waiters are patiently and happily take your order and serve the meat with a nice smile.

03

Nice Drinks

Fully licensed in-house beer is offered to boost up your dining experience. Or get the popular homemade drinks and soft drinks like guarana antartica or erva mate terere.
